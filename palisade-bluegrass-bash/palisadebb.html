Palisade Bluegrass Bash

2020 has us ready to party like they just invented the banjo and we are in West Virginia.

Check it out!

Get ready for some Pickin' and Drinkin'!

Amidst the beautiful paradise of western Colorado and the lush peach orchards, lies the cozy little town of palisade. Known for its welcoming atmosphere and delicious libations, there was a demand for shaking off the not so cool vibes of 2020. With such a love for entertaining and music, a few of the local establishments have decided to throw a little bit of BASH! A bluegrass bash! After calling in a few favors and realizing how much the world wants to hear live music, we've pulled together some incredible pickers and some seriously cool venues and we are ready to have some fun!
